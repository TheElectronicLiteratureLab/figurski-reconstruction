Figurski at Findhorn on Acid

by Richard Holeton

Introductions Dedication and Acknowledgements References and Resources

A mechanical pig, a commune in Scotland, the Holodeck, psychedelic drugs, a shower in a bathroom in a Florida trailer, Spam, cup-flipping, an international spy—all this and more are back for the 20th Anniversary version of Richard Holeton’s zany hypertext novel Figurski at Findhorn on Acid.

Originally published in 2001 by Eastgate Systems, Inc. with Storyspace software, this unique comedic hypertext has been re-created as an archival version in open web languages. Follow Figurski as he travels the universe in search of Rosellini’s 1737 Mechanical Pig.
